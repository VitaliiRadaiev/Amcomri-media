OUR BOARD

Amcomri Entertainment Inc Board Members

  • PAUL McGOWAN
    Non-Executive Chairman
    Paul McGowan is the Chairman of Amcomri LP, and was previously Chairman of the entertainment retailer HMV in the UK, Ireland and Canada.
  • LARRY HOWARD
    Interim Financial Officer
    Larry is a highly experienced finance director having previously filled that role at Dunnes Stores, Ireland’s largest retail group. He led the acquisition of Metrodome, Tartan Palisades and Hollywood Classics as well as the formation of Amcomri Film Partners and 101 International.
  • ROBERT PRICE
    Chief Executive Officer
    Robert is an experienced senior executive in media, entertainment and marketing. Most recently he was SVP & Managing Director for 20th Century Fox, prior to that he was UK CEO for Future Plc. He’s been creating and developing successful digital businesses and brands for over 20 years.
  • JONATHAN FORD
    Managing Director, Abacus Media Rights
    An experienced television and film sales executive, having managed the sales teams at Kew Media Distribution and Content Media, Jonathan previously worked for Sony Pictures, BT TV and Freeserve. He is now the Managing Director of Abacus Media Rights.
  • ANDY LYON
    CEO, 101 Films, 101 Films International
    Andy is a highly regarded UK-based entrepreneur with almost 30 years of experience in the film distribution business. He is a founder and CEO of 101 Films, 101 Films International and Hollywood Classics International.
  • EOGHAN BURKE
    Vice-President, Sales, 101 Films International
    A Dublin-based former Head of Sales at Sony Music, and previously managing sales and distribution for DreamWorks, Universal and Paramount Pictures, Eoghan is now Vice-President of International Sales at 101 Films International.
